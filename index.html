Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 21, 2025
Last Updated: September 21, 2025

Overview

Readdit Later is a Chrome extension that helps you save and export Reddit posts to Notion. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Important: All your data is stored locally in your browser and is only sent to Notion when you explicitly choose to export. We prioritize your privacy and data security.

Information We Collect

  • Reddit Posts: We temporarily store Reddit post data (title, content, URL, metadata) locally in your browser
  • Notion Authentication: We use OAuth to connect to your Notion account (we never store your Notion password)
  • Usage Data: Basic usage statistics to improve the extension

How We Use Your Information

  • Export Function: To export your saved Reddit posts to your chosen Notion database
  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally in your browser, not on our servers
  • Authentication: To connect securely to your Notion workspace

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your Reddit posts with third parties
  • Notion Integration: Data is only sent to Notion when you explicitly choose to export

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally in your browser. This means:
  • Local Only: All Reddit posts are stored locally in your browser
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your data on our servers
  • Notion Export: Data is sent directly to your Notion workspace when you export

Your Rights

  • Delete Data: You can delete all stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • Export Control: You choose what data to export and when
  • Revoke Access: You can disconnect Notion access at any time

Security

  • HTTPS: All connections use secure HTTPS encryption
  • OAuth: We use Notion's secure OAuth system for authentication
  • No Passwords: We never store your Notion or Reddit passwords

Changes

We may update this privacy policy periodically. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Changes will be posted with a new effective date
  • Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
